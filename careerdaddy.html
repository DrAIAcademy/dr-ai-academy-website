No. It is useful for freshers, job switchers, final-year students and working professionals.

No. CareerDaddy is for ethical interview preparation. Experts provide general role-based practice and feedback, not confidential employer data.

Yes. You can choose an expert, book a session, pay online and get a feedback dossier after the interview.

Basic tools are free. Advanced reports, expert interviews and premium features are paid.
